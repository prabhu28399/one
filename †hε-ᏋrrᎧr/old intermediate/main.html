BIPC

Intermediate with Biology, Physics, and Chemistry (BIPC) is a popular course of study for students who are interested in pursuing careers in healthcare, medicine, and life sciences. BIPC is an interdisciplinary program that covers a wide range of topics related to biology, physics, and chemistry.

CEC

Intermediate with Commerce, Economics, and Civics (CEC) is a popular course of study for students who are interested in pursuing careers in the fields of commerce, finance, economics, and related areas.
